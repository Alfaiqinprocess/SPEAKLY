Daily Conversations

Real-world English dialogues to help you communicate in everyday situations.

🔍
🎓 Campus

At Campus

Student A: Good morning. Is this seat available?
Student B: Yes, please have a seat.
Student A: Thank you.
Student B: You're welcome.
🍽️ Restaurant

At Restaurant

Customer: May I see the menu, please?
Waiter: Of course. Here it is.
Customer: Thank you.
💼 Interview

Job Interview

Interviewer: Tell me about yourself.
Applicant: My name is Abdullah. I am a student majoring in Software Engineering.
🛍️ Shopping

Shopping

Customer: How much is this shirt?
Seller: It costs ten dollars.

💡 Practice Tip

Read each dialogue aloud with a partner. Focus on natural pronunciation and intonation. Practicing with a friend helps you build confidence much faster than studying alone.
