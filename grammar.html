Grammar Tips

Master English grammar with clear formulas, structured examples, and interactive lessons.

📝
How to use these lessons

Click on any lesson card to expand it. Read the formula, study the examples, and try writing your own sentences.

01
Simple Present Tense
Used for habits, facts, and routines
▼
Formula
Subject + Verb 1 + Object
Examples
I study English every day.
She speaks English fluently.
They practice grammar at school.
He reads a book every morning.

💡 Note: For third-person singular (he/she/it), add -s or -es to the verb. Example: He speaks, She reads, It works.

02
Simple Past Tense
Used for completed actions in the past
▼
Formula
Subject + Verb 2 + Object
Examples
I visited Jakarta last week.
They watched a movie yesterday.
She studied hard for the exam.
He spoke English at the interview.

💡 Note: Regular verbs add -ed in past tense (study → studied, visit → visited). Irregular verbs change form (speak → spoke, watch → watched).

Quick Reference Chart

Tense Formula Usage Signal Words
Simple Present S + V1 + O Habits, facts, routines every day, always, usually
Simple Past S + V2 + O Completed past actions yesterday, last week, ago
